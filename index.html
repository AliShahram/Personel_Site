About Me

I am an aspiring entrepreneur and software engineer. As a full stack developer, I have worked on projects of different sizes taking on the roles of team leader and team player. As a team member, I have grown to cherish the opportunity to be involved in decision making and creative part of project planning, in addition to developing software. I believe, only then the team can tailor software solutions that best meets clients’ needs. I’ve familiarized myself with the usual tools and programming style within the organizations and learned to thrive by practicing adaptability and taking on different roles and responsibilities while staying dedicated to our shared goal.

I am micro-ambitious: I break down my goals and work on them one day at a time with passion and drive. A year ago, after observing the shortcomings of the transportation system in Richmond, IN, I founded Easy Connect, a social startup aimed at improving the public transportation in small college towns. With a small focused team, we all shared the success of each other. This personal investment allowed me to interact with a new, growing group in an environment that I had never experienced before. Since there was no hard deadlines, I kept the team and myself motivated by setting intermediate goals and developing efficient communication.

I am self-motivated and a reliable team player. I have extensive experience in analysis, coding and debugging, and my passion in entrepreneurship keeps me driven and proactive. Thank you for your time and I am looking forward to connecting with you in the future.
